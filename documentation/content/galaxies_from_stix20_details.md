# STIX 2.0 to MISP Galaxies mapping

STIX 2.0 SDOs such as `Attack Pattern`, `Course of Action`, `Intrusion Set`, `Malware`, `Threat Actor`, `Tool` and `Vulnerability` are imported as MISP Galaxy Clusters.
